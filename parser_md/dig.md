Domain information groper (Dig) parsers
=======================================

Parsers included in this module are:

DigDnssec - command ``/usr/bin/dig +dnssec . SOA``
--------------------------------------------------
DigEdns - command ``/usr/bin/dig +edns=0 . SOA``
------------------------------------------------
DigNoedns - command ``/usr/bin/dig +noedns . SOA``
--------------------------------------------------
